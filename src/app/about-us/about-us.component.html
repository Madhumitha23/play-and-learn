About us


We at Good Children Games understand the importance of a scientific approach in engaging kids through educational games. Our platform is designed to incorporate the fundamental pillars of learning, such as curiosity, attention, error feedback, and practice, based on neuroscientists recommendations. Unlike traditional games, our UI/UX is minimal with minimal distractions to keep children focused on solving questions or playing games.


Our goal is to adapt the games according to the player to sustain their curiosity as much as possible. This version is only our first step, and we are working on grades for effective feedback, and much more games to add. We rely on feedback and support from parents and teachers like you.
